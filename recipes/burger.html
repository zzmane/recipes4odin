Smashed Burger

Savor the seductive allure of a smashed burger, with each juicy patty boasting a caramelized crust that delivers an irresistible burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns

Instructions

  1. Preheat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. While the griddle is heating, divide the ground beef into small, equal-sized portions (usually 2 to 4 ounces each).
  3. Season each portion with salt and pepper, shaping them loosely into balls.
  4. Place a ball of ground beef onto the hot griddle and immediately press it down with a spatula to create a thin patty.
  5. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until the bottom develops a nice crust. Flip the patty and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes or until the desired level of doneness is reached.
  6. Optional: Add cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking for a classic cheeseburger.
  7. Toast the burger buns on the griddle for a minute or until they are lightly golden.
  8. Assemble your smashed burgers with your favorite toppings and condiments.
